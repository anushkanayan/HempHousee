EXPERIMENT

PROCESS
Date:17/04/2023
1.Cooking liquid comprising Water (H2O) + Sodium Hydroxide(NaOH)+ Sodium Carbonate(Na2CO3) was made at room temperature and stirred
2.Hemp fibers were immersed in the liquid by dividing it in 3 different beakers.
3.The beakers were heated for 4 hours at 95 degrees Celsius.
4.After 4 hours, the beakers were taken out and left aside to cool down.
5.After it has cooled down, the fibers were taken out and the cooking liquid (now turned black due to lignin) was separated and poured in a beaker.
6.The fibers were spread out on an aluminum sheet and allowed to air dry.
7.The cooking liquid, now called black liquor, was passed through a filter paper for 1st level filtration. No residue was left behind.
8.The black liquor was now passed through a ceramic filter and allowed to filter out slowly.
Date: 21/04/2023
9.The black liquor filters out finally, and a black layer containing lignin is left behind.
10.The filter is oven dried for 2 mins and the black layer (now moisture less) is scraped out of the filter and stored in powder form in a small box.
